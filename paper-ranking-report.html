Notes on the Collection

Heavy duplication

20 works appear as 42 files — 22 redundant copies. Byte-identical pairs (Graphical Model for Bask/Basketball), arXiv-vs-conference versions (Meta-Analytics), and three copies of the Three Dimensions of Rebounding and Felsen & Lucey Body Shots papers each. Scores are held identical across copies.

Basketball dominates

Of 165 unique works: 85 basketball, 26 NFL, 12 soccer, 9 baseball, 8 hockey, 7 college football, plus betting, cricket and multi-sport. The NBA optical-tracking era (2012–2019) is the single densest cluster — and the least reproducible.

Method & caveats

Scores come from reading the first ~2 pages of each PDF (title, abstract, intro, data section) — enough to judge contribution, data source and method, not to audit results. Eight scanned/slide-only PDFs were scored from the filename and known literature. Treat these as a fast, directional triage, not peer review.
